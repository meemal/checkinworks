About Check-In Works

Supporting autistic people and those with learning disabilities to build confidence, develop skills, and achieve their potential.

Our Story

Check-In Works was established to address a vital need in Perth & Kinross: providing meaningful work experience and skills training for autistic people and people with learning disabilities.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to develop their potential, gain confidence, and work towards independence. Our programmes are designed to support individuals at their own pace, in a safe and understanding environment.

Through our Giraffe Kitchen training programme and other initiatives, we've helped dozens of people develop practical skills, build self-esteem, and take positive steps towards employment and independent living.

Our Values

Inclusion

Creating a welcoming environment where everyone is valued and supported to participate fully.

Respect

Treating every individual with dignity and honoring their unique strengths and perspectives.

Empowerment

Supporting individuals to build confidence, make choices, and achieve their personal goals.

Excellence

Delivering high-quality support and continuously improving our services to better serve our community.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of staff and volunteers brings together expertise in autism support, learning disabilities, vocational training, and community development.

Experienced Staff

Our staff members are trained professionals with experience in supporting autistic people and people with learning disabilities. They understand the unique needs of each trainee and provide personalized support to help everyone succeed.

Committed Volunteers

Our volunteers play a crucial role in delivering our programmes. They bring enthusiasm, skills, and dedication to support our trainees and help create a welcoming, inclusive environment.
